A LITTLE INTRO

An artist is a person who creates art using their creative imagination and conscious skill. Art can include painting, sculpture, music, or writing. Artists often create objects that are beautiful, thought-provoking, and sometimes shocking. They may also strive to communicate ideas or feelings through their art.
